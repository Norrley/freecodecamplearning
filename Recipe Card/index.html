Savory Breakfast Casserole

Delicious breakfast casserole that's easy to make, and perfect for meal prep!

Ingredients:

  • 2 pounds of ground turkey
  • 1 can of beans (any)
  • 2 medium zucchini
  • 1 small yellow onion
  • 2 green peppers
  • 32 ounces of egg whites
  • 4 large eggs
  • 8 slices of bacon (cooked)
  • 2 cups of shredded cheddar cheese

Instructions:

  1. Heat saucepan on medium heat, add oil
  2. Dice the zucchini, peppers and onion, add to saucepan once heated. Sautee until halfway softened
  3. Preheat oven to 425 degrees
  4. Add ground turkey and can of beans to the saucepan with vegetables. Season and let cook until turkey is browned. Strain.
  5. Chop the cooked bacon into smaller peices, set aside.
  6. While turkey mixture is straining, in saucepan, add egg whites, eggs, chopped bacon and cheese. Whisk together.
  7. In a 9x11 baking dish, pour in turkey mixture, and flatten with a spatula to make an even layer.
  8. Slowly pour egg mixture over flattened turkey layer, making sure there is no overflow
  9. Use spatula to spread out any cheese or bacon peices that are clumped in one spot
  10. Place dish into the preheated oven and let bake for 45 minutes, or until mixture is no longer liquidy
  11. Remove once casserole is baked, and let cool fully before storing in the refrigerator. Good for 5 days.
